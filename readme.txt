Monoblog is a widget plugin that allows recordings of you reading your blogs aloud for the seeing/reading impaired or for people on the go.

== Description ==

Combines the awesome functionality of <a href="http://mediaelementjs.com/">MediaElement.js HTML5 media player</a> and <a href='http://www.plupload.com/'>Plupload</a> to enable you or the authors of your site to upload mp3s of people reading the text of your blogs.

Check out <a href="http://ownthenarrative.com/">OwnTheNarrative.com</a> for more information and examples.

###  Setup

After activating, while still in the admin panel, go to Appearance->Widgets. Then click and drag the Monoblog widget to whatever sidebar/area you want. NOTE: The sidebar/widget area must be visible in your posts in order for this to work.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `monoblog` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress

== Uploading ==

1. Record yourself reading your blog post and save it as an mp3. (Use Audacity or a similar program)
2. Navigate to your blog post in your browser
3. If you have at least author rights, you should see "Select Monoblog File". Give that a click.
4. Select your mp3 file.
5. Click Upload

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who can post a monoblog? =

Anyone with at least author rights can post a monoblog. Anyone with higher permissions has veto power over authors to moderate and curb abuse.

= How can I remove monoblog entries? =

Log into the admin interface. Go to Media->Monoblog to see the admin panel for this plugin. The only option available is "Remove".
